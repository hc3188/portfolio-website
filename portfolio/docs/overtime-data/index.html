Overtime at the New York City Housing Authority

By Harrison Connery

In Fiscal Year 2020, of the 10 city employees with the most overtime pay, eight of them worked at the New York City Housing Authority. Of those eight, seven are plumbers and one is an electrician. The findings come two years after the agency came under fire when it was discovered that a plumber there was the top city earner. That year, FY18, and the next, there were only two NYCHA employees among the top 10 overtime earners. In 2019, a new CEO was appointed to the agency. Why have these problems persisted and is there any wrongdoing?

The housing authority is one of the top overall overtime spenders.
